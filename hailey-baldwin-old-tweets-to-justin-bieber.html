We are here to reassure you that everyone went through their Justin Bieber "phase" at some point—even Hailey Baldwin, Bieber's future wife.

Ever since it was first reported that Bieber proposed to Baldwin (which he confirmed last night on Instagram), pieces from their long friendship and romance have been resurfacing. Some of the best are Baldwin's old fangirl tweets replying to him and re-posting his tweets dozens of times. She even called Justin's relationship with Selena Gomez "perfect."
