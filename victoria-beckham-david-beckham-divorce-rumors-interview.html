Victoria Beckham Speaks Out on Those Divorce Rumors, &amp; Discusses Marriage to David Beckham

 Posted on June 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  405 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • Victoria Beckham is setting the record straight regarding her marriage to soccer star David Beckham.
  • The successful designer has opened up in an interview with The Guardian's Weekend magazine, discussing the "frustrating" rumors that seem to continually circulate regarding her marriage.
  • Victoria and David married in July 1999, and have four children together—sons Brooklyn, Romeo, and Cruz, and daughter Harper.

Speaking to The Guardian's Weekend magazine, Victoria Beckham has opened up about her home life, putting to bed any split rumors that are still circulating. Explaining her extremely busy schedule, spent running her Victoria Beckham label, she said, "I never cook. I used to—when we lived in Spain, I used to cook a lot. But these days I don’t tend to get home till late, so dinner wouldn’t be ready till quite late. David’s a really good cook."

As well as cooking for her and the children, Victoria made it clear that any divorce rumors were just that—rumors: "It can get quite frustrating. But I leave it to my PR team. I don't get involved."

Earlier this month, David and Victoria were seen exhibiting some serious PDA at a London Fashion Week event, and it's clear that the Beckhams have a lot of love for one another.

Still, Victoria understands the fascination, seeing as her entire family has been in the public eye for many years now. She revealed to The Guardian's Weekend magazine, "People are interested in my personal life. And sometimes that works in my favor and sometimes it’s things that I don’t like. I’m not going to let it get me down.”

But when the interviewer tried to draw her on David, Victoria stood firm: "You do have to be quite controlling because people do believe what they read, and when it’s completely fabricated, that’s really annoying."
