Is Brittany Mahomes pregnant for a third time? Brittany Matthews, a sports star, was born on September 1, 1995. She played professional football in Europe before launching her own fitness firm. Is Brittany Mahomes Pregnant for 3rd Time?

Brittany Mahomes clarified pregnancy rumours on Instagram. Despite speculation, she stated that she was not expecting her third child. During an Instagram Q&A, someone inquired if she was pregnant, and she answered: “I’m not sure where this rumour started, but no, I am not pregnant.”

She and her husband, Patrick Mahomes, have a two-year-old daughter, Sterling Skye, and a nine-month-old son, Patrick “Bronze” Lavon Mahomes III.

When asked if they planned to have additional children, Brittany responded: “We aren’t sure, honestly!”  So they haven’t decided to have more children.

Meet Brittany Matthews’ Husband

Patrick Mahomes and Brittany Matthews are engaged on August 1, 2020. The proposal was really sweet, and it occurred only one day after Matthews turned 25.

It happened in a special VIP box at Arrowhead Stadium in Kansas City, Missouri, immediately after the football team received their Super Bowl 54 rings. Brittany revealed the proposal setup on Instagram, which included gorgeous white flowers and bright letters that read, “Will You Marry Me.” She wrote, “So, this happened today!”

Patrick, who plays in the NFL, revealed the engagement ring he gave Brittany. It was a rare diamond with a double band, and he captioned it “Ring SZN” on Instagram.

After she answered yes, they had a candlelit meal to celebrate their engagement. Brittany expressed her happiness on Instagram, writing: “My heart is so full. I couldn’t imagine this day being any more perfect!”

Brittany Matthews and Patrick Mahomes Welcome Their Children to The Family

Patrick Mahomes and Brittany Matthews announced their first pregnancy in October 2020, just two months after getting engaged, with the baby due in February 2021.

Brittany posted the news on Instagram, saying: “Mom & Dad, taking a small detour to the wedding.”  They were overjoyed with their first baby. Patrick even stated on a radio broadcast that he hoped the kid would arrive shortly after the Super Bowl so that he could attend the delivery.

Sterling Skye, their daughter, was born on February 20 after Patrick’s second Super Bowl appearance. Patrick shared a snapshot of their baby child gripping Brittany’s finger. It wasn’t long before their little one attended her first sporting event.

They uploaded photos of Sterling attending a Kansas City Royals baseball game on Opening Day 2021, where she wore a customised Mahomes shirt. She often accompany her mother to see Patrick play football. Just two months after their wedding, the Mahomes announced even more exciting news.

They stated that they planned to have a second kid. They announced the announcement on Instagram, with photographs of Sterling holding a letter board that read, “Big Sister Duties Coming Soon.”

They discovered they were expecting a son at a gender reveal party in June 2022. He was born on November 28, 2022, and was called Patrick “Bronze” Lavon Mahomes III after his father.

Even though he was very little, Brittany posted a video on Instagram of their newborn watching Patrick play football on TV, and this was a big game that brought them to the Super Bowl.
