Chris Eubank Jr. girlfriend-British professional boxer, Chris Eubank Jr. was born on September 18th, 1989 in Hove, East Sussex in the United Kingdom.

Eubank was born to the former professional boxer, Chris Eubank and Karron Suzanne Stephen-Martin. He shares the same parents with his three other siblings; Sebastian Eubank, Emily Eubank and Joseph Eubank.

He attended Brighton College for two years, Shoreham College for one, and Spring Valley High School in Las Vegas for two. In the reality television program At Home with the Eubanks, Eubank appeared alongside his father.

Eubank and his brother Sebastian relocated to the US at the age of 16 to live with their guardian, Irene Hutton. According to their mother Karron, it was a formal adoption that gave the two boys dual citizenship without the requirement for marriage and improved their chances of becoming professional athletes.

In 2007, Eubank began his amateur career. He won his sixth amateur fight, making him the 165-pound weight division’s Amateur Golden Gloves Champion for the State of Nevada.

He won his ninth amateur fight, making him the Western States’ Amateur Golden Glove Champion for his weight class. In the 2008 National Golden Gloves, Eubank had a 1-1 record. He finished his amateur career with a 24-2 record.

Chris Eubank Jr career

In 2011, Eubank signed a contract with promoter Mick Hennessy and became a professional. His father was his tutor, as was his father’s trainer Ronnie Davies. In the following three years, Eubank had an 18-0 record with 13 knockouts.
